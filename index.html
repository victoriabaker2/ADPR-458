About Me

Nice to Meet You!

Hello, my name is Victoria Baker, and I'm a writer. copywriter, creative, storyteller, word wizard, content connoisseur, etc, etc. I am a soon to be graduate at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, majoring in broadcasting and minoring in film studies. With my editorial and broadcast production background, I have robust experience in script writing for audio and video, content ranging from news stories and articles to creative television pilot pitches. With all of my content, storytelling and storyytelling devices are the root of it all. Check out some of my work below! If you like what you see, send me a message, and let's create together!
